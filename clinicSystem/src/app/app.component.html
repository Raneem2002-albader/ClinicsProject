Cardiology

Our AI supports early detection of cardiovascular conditions by analyzing symptoms and medical imaging. ICURE helps users identify signs of heart-related issues and connect with cardiologists for expert evaluation and treatment plans.

Neurology

ICURE uses AI models to assist in detecting neurological disorders such as migraines, strokes, or seizures. By evaluating CT/MRI scans and reported symptoms, we provide fast insights and connect users with neurology specialists.

Hepatology

Monitor and manage liver health with ICURE’s intelligent tools. The platform detects signs of liver dysfunction, supports early-stage diagnosis, and helps patients understand when to consult a hepatologist.

Pediatrics

Parents can use ICURE to analyze children’s symptoms and receive safe, AI-generated health assessments. The platform supports common pediatric concerns and offers direct consultation options with pediatric doctors.

Eye Care

ICURE supports early detection of eye-related conditions such as glaucoma or retinal issues through symptom analysis and imaging support. Easily consult with eye specialists for next steps.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is ICURE and how does it work?

ICURE is an AI-powered healthcare platform that analyzes symptoms and medical images to predict possible diseases and provide treatment recommendations.

Is ICURE a replacement for a doctor?

No, ICURE is a supportive tool. It provides early insights and connects users with specialized doctors for expert review.

Can I upload my medical scans like X-rays or MRIs?

Yes, ICURE allows you to upload X-ray, CT, and MRI images for AI-powered interpretation.

Does ICURE support Arabic and other languages?

Yes, ICURE is designed for both Arabic and English users, with plans to support more languages.

How accurate is the AI diagnosis?

ICURE's AI is trained on medical datasets and reviewed by medical experts, offering over 90% accuracy in supported conditions.

Do I need a doctor to use the platform?

No. You can start with AI analysis yourself and optionally consult with a doctor through the platform.
